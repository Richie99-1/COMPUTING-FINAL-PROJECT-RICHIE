Student Regulations

Follow school rules — Respect others and take responsibility!

CATEGORIES OF OFFENSES

1. MILD OFFENSES

  • Disrupting class order, including during online learning sessions.
  • Arriving late to school or to class.
  • Failing to bring the necessary materials for learning.
  • Using school facilities outside school hours without permission.
  • Wearing inappropriate or excessive accessories.
  • Eating in school areas outside of designated mealtimes without permission.
  • Turning on or using mobile phones during school hours without permission.
  • Bringing or playing games or sports equipment without permission.

2. MODERATE OFFENSES

(or repeated mild offenses)

  • Engaging in buying or selling transactions on school premises.
  • Failing to wear the proper school uniform.
  • Causing damage to school facilities (must repair or replace items).
  • Leaving school during active hours without permission.
  • Engaging in bullying — verbal, physical, written, or visual.

Bullying is intentional, repeated behavior that harms others and involves an imbalance of power.

3. SEVERE OFFENSES

(or repeated moderate offenses)

  • Being involved in physical fights.
  • Cheating during exams.
  • Bringing sharp or dangerous objects.
  • Possessing or consuming alcoholic beverages or cigarettes.
  • Committing criminal acts inside or outside the school.

NOTE

If any violation not listed above occurs, the school may address it according to policy.
